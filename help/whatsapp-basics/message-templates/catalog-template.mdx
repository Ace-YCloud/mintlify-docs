# Catalog Template

> **Info**
>
> Catalog templates currently only support API creation and sending

Catalog templates belong to marketing templates and allow you to fully showcase your products on WhatsApp. Catalog templates display the thumbnail title image and custom body of the products you select, along with fixed text headers and fixed text subtitles.

![](/images/gitbook/image-459-da8429ed.png)

When customers click the **View Catalog** button in the Catalog template message, your product catalog will appear in WhatsApp.

![](/images/gitbook/image-460-9eb87022.png)
