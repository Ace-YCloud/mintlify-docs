# Shopify

Bind your Shopify store to your YCloud account to synchronize store customer data and order data, helping you better manage your store.

## Binding Store Steps

## Step 1: Click to Install

Entry: Integrations>[Shopify](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/app/integrations/all)

Click Install

![](/images/gitbook/image-502-8cca84c2.png)

Review the agreement, and after confirming it is correct, click the Install button.

![](/images/gitbook/image-503-255f25ff.png)

## Step 2: Bind Store

After redirecting to the Shopify app store, click to install the software.

![](/images/gitbook/image-504-bdb773b1.png)
