# Freshdesk

Freshdesk is a ticketing system. After linking your Freshdesk account, you can directly create Freshdesk tickets in the Inbox workspace.


## Step1: Install YCloud app on Freshdesk app store.

Login to your Freshdesk account, visit Admin page, search App and click the APPs in Support Operations.

![](/images/gitbook/image-125-bf305d12.png)


Find YCloud app to install


## Step2: Connect YCloud account to the Freshdesk account.

![](/images/gitbook/image-126-f55b7c2c.png)

Find your Admin key and Freshdesk URL on profile sttings

![](/images/gitbook/image-127-9ad1a623.png)

![](/images/gitbook/image-129-966b018e.png)

## Step4: Create your ticket on Inbox

Visit [Inbox](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/app/inbox/chat) on YCloud.

When you need to create a ticket for issue follow-up during customer communication, you can quickly open the customer information and create a ticket in the Freshdesk section.

![](/images/gitbook/image-131-18b908d8.png)

Fill in the information and click submit.

![](/images/gitbook/image-132-c3232289.png)
