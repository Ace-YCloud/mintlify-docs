# Inbox Introduction

## Overview

YCloud Inbox is a real-time chat tool based on WhatsApp, allowing customer service and sales teams to establish closer connections with customers, easily handle inquiries, solve problems, and collect feedback! Inbox provides detailed data analysis reports to help you make data-driven management and decisions.
