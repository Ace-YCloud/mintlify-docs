# WhatsApp Calling in Inbox

To help businesses communicate more effectively with customers, WhatsApp voice calling has been integrated into the Inbox. For an overview of WhatsApp Calling, please refer to:

[overview.md](/help/calling/overview)
This article focuses on how to make and receive voice calls with customers directly from the Inbox.


## Invite Customers to Call in

When text messages are not sufficient during a conversation, you can invite the customer to start a voice call, allowing you to resolve issues faster and more efficiently.

> **Info**
>
> Inbound calls are free of charge.

### 1. Send a Call Invitation

Entry point:

Click WhatsApp Calling > Ask customer to call

![](/images/gitbook/image-811-66e5eb87.png)

Enter the reason for the call. After confirming the information, click Send.

![](/images/gitbook/image-813-992a5cc2.png)

Once sent, the customer will receive a call invitation message on their phone, as shown below. The customer can initiate the call by tapping the Call button.

![](/images/gitbook/image-814-3e1b9011.png)

#### 2. Answer an Incoming Call

When the customer starts a voice call using the invitation link you sent, you will see a notification in the Inbox.

![](/images/gitbook/image-815-02202906.png)

Click Answer to accept the call and start the conversation.

## Make an Outbound Call

Before making an outbound call, you must have permission to place calls using the business phone number. Please refer to the [relevant documentation](https://helpdocs.ycloud.com/help-center/calling/overview#outbound-calling-permissions) for more details.


⚠️ Outbound voice calls are chargeable.

For pricing details, please visit: https://www.ycloud.com/pricing#price-table


### 1. Request for Calling permission

Entry point:

Click WhatsApp Calling > Call out

![](/images/gitbook/image-816-7de80a71.png)


YCloud will guide you to first send a call request message to the customer.

![](/images/gitbook/image-817-7e41d9a6.png)

### 2. Receive Authorization

Once the customer grants permission, you will receive a system notification in the Inbox.

![](/images/gitbook/image-818-b235b008.png)


### 3. Start the Ountbound Call

After authorization is granted, click the Call out button again to initiate the outbound voice call.

![](/images/gitbook/image-819-069f5186.png)

### View Call Records

Open the Call Records panel on the right-hand side of the Inbox to view detailed call logs, including call status and duration.

![](/images/gitbook/image-820-675353c0.png)
