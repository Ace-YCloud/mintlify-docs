# Billing and Recharge

## Recharge

Supports WeChat and credit card payments.

Customers bound to Shopify stores only support Shopify payments.

**Recharge Entry**: Bottom left avatar > Billing > [Buy credits](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/app/billing/buyCredits), or move the mouse to the balance at the bottom left and click the arrow to the right of the balance.

![](/images/gitbook/image-19-1-88b9b7ca.png)

## Billing Details

The billing page includes all consumption, recharge, gift amounts, and other software deduction records within the account.

Billing Entry: Bottom left avatar > [Billing](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/app/billing/overview)

Click the view icon for Country in the bill to see more detailed billing data.

Click the download button to export the bill.

![](/images/gitbook/image-16-1-d4a96e91.png)

## Daily Consumption Notification

Sends the previous day's consumption data to the recipient daily.

Configuration Entry: Bottom left avatar > Billing > [Notifications](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/app/billing/notification)

![](/images/gitbook/image-17-1-f2cad354.png)
