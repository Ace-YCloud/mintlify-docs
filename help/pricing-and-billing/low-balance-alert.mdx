# Low Balance Alert

To avoid sending failures due to insufficient funds, it is strongly recommended to enable balance alerts. Promptly recharge your account to continue service.


Configuration Entry: Bottom left avatar > Settings > General settings >  [Notifications](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/app/billing/notification)
