# CTWA Introduction


CTWA (Click to WhatsApp Ads) is an ad format that sends traffic directly into a WhatsApp conversation.&#x20;

With YCloud, you can centrally receive visitor messages from different ad platforms, follow up with leads, and continuously improve ad performance through conversion reporting.

### What Is CTWA

After seeing an ad, users can click the CTA in the ad and go directly to WhatsApp to start a conversation with your business. Compared with the traditional path of “ad > landing page > form submission or callback,” CTWA shortens the conversion path and is especially suitable for businesses focused on consultation, lead generation, and sales conversion.

A typical CTWA journey looks like this:

* A user sees your ad on an ad platform
* The user clicks a CTA such as “Send Message” or “Chat on WhatsApp”
* WhatsApp opens automatically
* The user starts a conversation with your business right away

### How CTWA Differs from Traditional Ad Flows

The core value of CTWA is that it reduces the steps between “clicking an ad” and “starting a conversation,” so users can enter a conversation faster.

> **Info**
>
> Common benefits of CTWA include:
>
> * Shorter conversion paths by sending ad traffic directly to WhatsApp conversations
> * Faster response to high-intent customer inquiries, with less user drop-off
> * Automatic access to the WhatsApp contact information of users who start a conversation
> * Continuous ad optimization through conversation data and conversion event reporting

### What YCloud Helps You Do in CTWA Scenarios

With YCloud, you can connect ad platforms, WhatsApp conversation handling, lead management, and conversion reporting into one workflow.

YCloud helps you:

* Receive WhatsApp conversations from ad platforms in one place
* View lead sources and assign follow-up in YCloud Inbox
* Record ad source information and identify which ad a contact came from
* Report conversation or conversion events back to ad platforms based on rules
* View ad performance, leads, and event details in YCloud

### What You Should Prepare Before Getting Started

Before entering a platform-specific setup flow, it is recommended that you confirm the following basics are ready:

* A usable ad account
* A WhatsApp Business Number that has been connected to YCloud
* A sales or support team that can respond to WhatsApp inquiries in time
