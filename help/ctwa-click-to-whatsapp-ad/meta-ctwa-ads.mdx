# Meta CTWA Ads

After completing this setup, you will be able to run Facebook or Instagram ads that send users directly to WhatsApp, bring leads into conversation faster, and use YCloud to receive leads, report conversion events, and review ad performance.&#x20;

> **Info**
>
> If you are not familiar with CTWA yet, we recommend reading this first:
>
> * [CTWA Introduction](https://helpdocs.ycloud.com/help-center/ctwa-click-to-whatsapp-ad/ctwa-introduction)

### What Meta CTWA Ads Are

Meta CTWA ads are ads shown on Facebook or Instagram that let users click a CTA and go directly into a WhatsApp conversation with your business.

For businesses, the value of this format is not just getting a click. The real value is that users can enter a conversation faster, while YCloud helps you handle the conversation, follow up with leads, and optimize later conversions.

### Key Setup Relationships in the Meta Flow

In the Meta CTWA flow, the relationship between your ad account, Facebook Page, Instagram account, and WhatsApp Business Number directly affects whether the ad can be created and delivered successfully.

Before you begin, make sure these core assets are available:

* A usable Meta ad account
* A WhatsApp Business Number already connected to YCloud
* A Facebook Page for ad delivery
* The necessary permissions for the operator managing these assets

### Account Relationships You Should Confirm Before Launch

In Meta CTWA, the required asset relationships vary slightly depending on where the ad will run.

### What to Prepare Before You Start

Before moving into the formal setup, it is recommended that you confirm the following items are ready.

#### Meta-side Preparation

* A usable Meta ad account
* Permission to manage the target Business Manager, ad account, and Facebook Page
* A Facebook Page for ad delivery
* If you plan to run ads on Instagram, make sure the corresponding Instagram professional account is ready and available

#### YCloud-side Preparation

* A YCloud account you can log in to normally
* A WhatsApp Business Number already connected to YCloud
* A sales or support team that can respond to WhatsApp inquiries in time

If you have not yet created and connected your WhatsApp business account in YCloud, you can start here:

* [Create WABA via Embedded Sign-up](https://helpdocs.ycloud.com/help-center/whatsapp-accounts-management/create-a-whatsapp-api-account/create-waba-via-embedded-sign-up)

### Setup Flow Overview

The full Meta CTWA setup can be divided into the following 3 stages:

1. Connect your Meta ad account in YCloud
2. Create a Click to WhatsApp ad in Meta Ads Manager
3. Configure conversion reporting and review ad and conversion data in Meta and YCloud

### Step-by-Step Guides

#### Step 1: Connect Your Meta Ad Account

After your Meta ad account is authorized in YCloud, YCloud can read ad-related data and support later conversion reporting and data review.

* [Connect Meta Ad Account](https://helpdocs.ycloud.com/help-center/ctwa-click-to-whatsapp-ad/facebook-ads/connect-facebook-ad-account)

#### Step 2: Create a Click to WhatsApp Ad

In Ads Manager, you will complete the ad objective selection, Page-to-WhatsApp setup, ad set configuration, and ad creative setup.

* [Create Click to WhatsApp Ad (CTWA)](https://helpdocs.ycloud.com/help-center/ctwa-click-to-whatsapp-ad/facebook-ads/create-click-to-whatsapp-ad-ctwa)

#### Step 3: Configure Conversion Reporting and Review Data

After the ad goes live, you can continue by configuring conversion reporting rules in YCloud and reviewing conversations, conversions, and performance data in both Meta and YCloud.

* [Meta Ads：Traffic Delivery,Conversion Reporting](https://helpdocs.ycloud.com/help-center/ctwa-click-to-whatsapp-ad/facebook-ads/conversion-api-capi)
