# Create/Import Contacts

## **Single Manual Import**

### Step 1

Log in to your [YCloud account](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/entry/login), navigate to Contact > Contact list > + New contacts

![](/images/gitbook/image-36-1-6a231ab9.png)

### Step 2

Click + New contacts > Add a new contact, fill in the contact information > Click Submit

![](/images/gitbook/image-37-1-0f6511b2.png)

![](/images/gitbook/image-38-1-3c145727.png)

## **Bulk Manual Import**

### Step 1

Log in to your [YCloud account](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/entry/login), navigate to Contact > Contact list > + New contacts

![](/images/gitbook/image-39-1-a92da287.png)

### Step 2

Click + New contacts > Upload a file, upload the contact document; click “Next” to upload

![](/images/gitbook/image-40-1-db655121.png)

![](/images/gitbook/image-42-1-da16fb9c.png)
