# Contact Tag Settings

> **Info**
>
> Regarding tag settings, we support two methods for adding tags. You can choose either of the following methods:
>
> 1. You can view and edit tags at Settings > Tags.
> 2. You can tag users in the Contact list section, or you can tag users in the Inbox tool.

## Add tags in Settings

### Step 1

Log in to your [YCloud account](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/entry/login), navigate to Contact > Settings > Tags.

![](/images/gitbook/image-282-1f970afe.png)

### Step 2

Click Add Tag, add a new tag, and click Save to complete the operation. After successful addition, you can edit and delete the tag.

![](/images/gitbook/image-283-6922bb73.png)

![](/images/gitbook/image-284-a6e0fa84.png)

## Adding in Contact list

### Step 1

Log in to your [YCloud account](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/entry/login), navigate to Contact > Contact list > Add tag.

![](/images/gitbook/image-287-bfd33cb3.png)

### Step 2

Click Add tag, enter the tag you want to add, and click Create new tag to complete the operation.

![](/images/gitbook/image-286-e52f84f2.png)
