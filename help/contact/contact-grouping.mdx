# Contact Grouping

## Step 1 — Find the entrance to create a new segment

You can find the entry for creating a new segment through the following path:&#x20;

Log in to your [YCloud account](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/entry/login) > navigate to Contact > Click "Add filter".

![](/images/gitbook/image-773-3e805adf.png)

## Step 2 — Set filter conditions

Add the appropriate filter criteria based on your segmentation needs.\
For example, group contacts whose country type is China, you need to follow the steps below.

#### 1.Select filter type

👉Select "Country"

![](/images/gitbook/image-775-12eac1d2.png)

#### 2.Edit filter

👉Select "China"

![](/images/gitbook/image-776-5e94c246.png)

#### 3.Save Segment

👉Click "Done" and the list will display the filtered contact data

![](/images/gitbook/image-777-bbb0f549.png)

👉Click "Save Segment", add a segment name, and save the segment. The segment will be displayed in the contact list on the left.

![](/images/gitbook/image-778-a3445d17.png)

![](/images/gitbook/image-779-b955fcc6.png)

>
