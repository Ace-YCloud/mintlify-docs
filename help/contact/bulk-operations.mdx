# Bulk Operations

## **Batch Import Contacts**

[Click to access](/help/contact/create-import-contacts) the batch import contacts guide

## Batch Export Contacts

Log in to your [YCloud account](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/entry/login), navigate to Contact > Export, to batch export contacts

![](/images/gitbook/image-71-ffec5233.png)

## Batch Filter Contacts

Log in to your [YCloud account](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/entry/login), navigate to Contact > Click the icon in the upper right corner: "Set Displayed attributes". Select the properties you want to view, or hide the properties you don't want to view for now.

![](/images/gitbook/image-780-0263e769.png)
