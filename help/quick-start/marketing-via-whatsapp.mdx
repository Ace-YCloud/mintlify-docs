# Marketing via WhatsApp

> **Info**
>
> WhatsApp, a widely used messaging app around the world, has become an important tool in the field of marketing. It is popular for its immediacy, interactivity and wide user base. Marketing through WhatsApp can not only improve brand visibility, effectively reduce marketing costs, and achieve more accurate targeting. Therefore, this direct, secure and efficient communication mode opens up new ways for enterprises to expand their market and brand building, and is a powerful channel to connect enterprises and customers and promote business growth.

> **Here's how to get started marketing via WhatsApp**

## Create automated marketing strategies

Automated Marketing editor with YCloud platform (Journey)

Journey is an automated marketing tool that helps businesses trigger a series of actions based on customer behavior, enabling instant marketing push for specific customer behavior, such as automatically sending a payment reminder when a customer abandons a settlement payment.

In the YCloud platform, you can easily configure such an automated marketing process to automate and personalize marketing messages and improve conversion results.

![](/images/gitbook/image-666-dcb3fe2c.png)

### **Step 1: Create Journey**

* [Log in to your YCloud account](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/app/getStarted) > [Journey](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/app/journey/list) > Create Journey

![](/images/gitbook/image-668-0caa982b.png)

### **Step 2: Add Trigger**

The Trigger is the Trigger for this Journey, and all conditions in the trigger must be met before it can be successfully triggered.

![](/images/gitbook/image-669-fd6c8bcd.png)

### **Step 3: Design process**

Drag and drop components and connect them to design a process for execution based on your business scenario. For details about component functions, see Component description

For example, when a customer does not complete the payment after 10 minutes of placing an order, it will automatically send a reminder message, and if the payment is completed during the period, it will not be sent, and the conversion rate of customers will be improved through this automated marketing method.&#x20;

![](/images/gitbook/image-670-005c667e.png)

### **Step 4: Active and Save**

After completing the process setting, click the Save button in the upper right corner, set the status of Journey to active, and click Save.

![](/images/gitbook/image-671-ca46c039.png)

If the component Settings are incorrect, you can view the error message in the upper right corner of the red box and click the Check button in the upper right corner to check the Settings.

![](/images/gitbook/image-672-4948f382.png)

Click to view [Create a Journey](https://helpdocs.ycloud.com/help-center/journey/chuang-jian-yi-ge-journey)

## Best practice

### **Through Contact to achieve customer refined group marketing**

When using the Campaign function, select Contact segment users for targeted delivery to pave the way for precise marketing

![](/images/gitbook/image-673-d6fd5763.png)

![](/images/gitbook/image-674-78139bc4.png)

Click to view [Contact Grouping](https://helpdocs.ycloud.com/help-center/contact/lian-xi-ren-fen-zu)

### **Unsubscribe is allowed, respecting the user's choice**

When enterprises send marketing messages, some customers are easy to dislike complaints, resulting in the risk of the account being blocked. In order to prevent blocking, enterprises should add an unsubscribe button when sending mass messages to facilitate users to unsubscribe. After customers join the unsubscribe list, enterprises can choose to filter these customers when sending messages to reduce the probability of complaints.

![](/images/gitbook/image-675-8b074da7.png)

Click to view [Automatically Add Customers to Unsubscribe List](https://helpdocs.ycloud.com/help-center/chatbot/zui-jia-shi-jian/zi-dong-jiang-ke-hu-tian-jia-dao-tui-ding-lie-biao)

### **MM Lite API authorization to optimize message reach**

[MM Lite API](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/app/integrations/mmLiteApi) is Meta's newly launched interface for sending WhatsApp marketing template messages. Compared with Cloud API, MM Lite API has a higher user reach rate and reading rate. Its marketing messages are optimized by Meta AI system to more accurately reach customers who prefer marketing messages and improve marketing effects. Messages sent using the MM Lite API can be tracked in the advertising system, and if the website has used Pixel burying, it can monitor the effectiveness of marketing messages in the advertising account.

* Only applicable to companies with registered addresses in Brazil, India, Indonesia, Mexico, Chile, Peru, Singapore, Hong Kong, USA.

![](/images/gitbook/image-676-2fa2c1b3.png)

Click to view [How to use the MM Lite API](https://helpdocs.ycloud.com/help-center/integrations/mm-lite-api)
