# Create WhatsApp Pay Template

> **Info**
>
> Please note: Your YCloud account can only create templates with WhatsApp Pay links after the BM account has been successfully linked to the third-party payment account.

## Step 1:

Log in to your YCloud account > Templates > Add Template

![](/images/gitbook/image-591-440e2c88.png)

## Step 2:

Select the template type you want to create with WhatsApp Pay. Then, follow the prompts on the page to fill in the required information within the message template, and click "Submit" to complete the process.

![](/images/gitbook/image-592-615e8c1c.png)
