# Phone Number Profile Settings

The phone number profile includes descriptions such as profile photo and company information.

## Step 1: Access Profile Settings

Go to the [WhatsApp accounts](https://www.ycloud.com/console/#/app/dashboard/account) page and click on the Settings of the phone number.

![](/images/gitbook/image-179-1-356dcad4.png)

*WhatsApp accounts*

## Step 2: Edit Profile

Enter the Profile to edit the information. After completion, click the save button below.

![](/images/gitbook/image-772-9400805e.png)
