# Chat Links

Generate shareable links, QR codes, or web chat widgets for numbers.

Setup guidance: Home > WhatsApp accounts > Phone number settings > Chat Links

## Key Points

**Simplified Communication Process**\
Typically, to chat via WhatsApp app, you need to know the other party's phone number, save it on your mobile device, and then search for it to start a conversation. With YCloud's growth tools, this process is simplified, as customers can initiate a WhatsApp chat just by clicking a link or scanning a QR code.

**Quick Replies**\
With simple setup, you can preset custom messages and automatically reply quickly, supporting a large number of customers accessing simultaneously. To enable this feature, you need to connect your WhatsApp account in YCloud.

**Multiple Ways to Reach Users**\
You can generate QR codes, links, or web chat widgets for various scenarios such as websites, apps, landing pages, and offline stores. This allows you to capture potential customer.

**Compliance in Collecting User Information**\
Customers who chat with you through YCloud Chat Links are automatically added to your Contacts. You can view and edit their information, such as the time added and the last conversation time, and use YCloud mass messaging tool to create suitable marketing campaigns for targeted customers.

## Usage Scenarios

**Website Customer Service Entry:** Insert a chat widget on your website to allow customers to quickly find the WhatsApp customer service entry.

**Distributing Flyers:** Print QR codes on flyers and distribute them. Interested customers can scan the code to quickly obtain leads.

**Scan to Join Membership:** Place a membership QR code in your offline store. Customers can join your membership system via WhatsApp and successfully leave their membership details.

**Landing Page Inquiry:** Add a WhatsApp button on the landing page. Interested customers can click the button to connect with you via WhatsApp, without worrying about incorrect contact information, directly obtaining the target customer's WhatsApp account.
